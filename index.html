      🛡️ Safety Tips

      • ❌ Never share OTP or passwords
        ▼
        Scammers can use your OTP to instantly access your actual bank account and steal your funds. Official sources will never ask for them.
      • 🔗 Avoid clicking unknown links
        ▼
        Obscure URLs can silently install malware directly to your device or route you to identical fake websites to capture passwords.
      • 🆔 Verify sender identity natively
        ▼
        Always search for the official customer service endpoints online rather than blindly evaluating contact data placed inside random texts.
      • 📱 Enable robust 2FA
        ▼
        Always try to deploy explicit Authenticator applications (like Google Auth) to completely mitigate attacker endpoints even if passwords leak.
